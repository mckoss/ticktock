Get Started

Simple as a Post-It Note

No complicated menus or wizards. Just type your to-dos, tag them Twitter-style to assign them or add keyword tags. Check them off when you're done.

Analytics, not accounting

Spend your time working, not counting your bamboo. PandaTask measures how long it takes you to get things done and helps you understand how you spend your time, without requiring lots of data entry.

Teamwork

Pandas like working in teams but don't like being told what to do. PandaTask is useful for personal productivity but gets even better when Pandas work together. Your manager can monitor the team's progress without nagging you to track your tasks.
